Courses We Offer

The courses offered in our institution include CSE,IT,ECE,EEE,Mechanical,Civil,Chemical.

CSE and IT

The RVR&JCCE introduced the undergraduate program in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) in 1994. Students of this department are working in top MNC's like TCS, CTS, Infosys, Oracle, Accenture, IBM, Wipro, CISCO etc.
Information Technology program started in 1998 in this college so as to meet the rapidly expanding demand for graduates with interdisciplinary skills for application across the whole range of computer and information based industries. A graduate study was introduced in 2013 in the Computer Science and Technology.

ECE and EEE

The department of ECE came in to existence in 1985 immediately when the institute was founded. It was started with an intake of 40 which was subsequently raised to 180 over a period of time.Our students are well placed in companies like TCS, CTS, IBM, Oracle, Qualcom, Texas Instruments BSNL, BEL, ECIL, ISRO, DRDO, AMD are just few to mention
The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering was established in the academic year 1994 - 1995 with an intake of 60 students. The intake is enhanced to 120 from the academic year 2004 -2005 and to 180 from the academic year 2013-2014.

Mechanical and Civil

The Department was established in 1985. It started an U.G. course in Mechanical Engineering in the same year. This course had been accredited by N.B.A. and awarded 'A' Grade for three years in May, 1999, 'A' Grade for five years in May, 2002. The Institution was Accredited by NAAC with "A" grade IN 2014.
The Department was well established and running successfully with an intake of 180 Students. Civil Engineering is a challenging discipline. It encompasses all important aspects of Modern Technology. In Buildings, Airports, Railways, Irrigation Projects and Civil Engineers have been playing a leading role.
